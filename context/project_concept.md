1. Project Description:
Fluentia is a web-based AI communication coaching platform designed to help users improve
their speaking confidence and communication skills through interactive scenario-based
conversations with an AI agent.
Many individuals experience difficulty expressing themselves clearly in situations such as casual
conversations, academic discussions, job interviews, and professional meetings. This challenge
often occurs due to limited opportunities to practice speaking in realistic environments.
Fluentia addresses this problem by providing users with an AI-powered communication coach
that simulates real-life conversations through scenario-based roleplay.
Users can select different communication scenarios and interact with an AI agent that responds
dynamically during conversations. Instead of focusing on complex real-time analysis, the system
emphasizes post-conversation feedback and performance tracking to ensure a reliable and
achievable implementation.
To enhance learning and improvement, Fluentia provides structured feedback and performance
evaluation after each interaction.
Key features of the system include:
• Scenario-Based AI Conversations
Users engage in interactive roleplay conversations based on real-life situations such as job
interviews, classroom discussions, and casual interactions.
• AI-Powered Conversation Feedback
After each session, the system analyzes the conversation and provides feedback, highlighting
strengths, areas for improvement, and actionable suggestions.
• Communication Scoring System
The platform generates a communication score based on metrics such as clarity, conciseness,
response relevance, and filler word usage.

• Communication Progress Dashboard
Users can track their improvement over time through a visual dashboard that displays
performance trends and past session results.
• Conversation History Tracking
The system stores past conversations, allowing users to review their responses and monitor
progress.
• AI Coach Persona
Users can select different AI personalities depending on the scenario, such as a strict
interviewer, supportive coach, or casual conversational partner. Each persona adjusts its tone and
interaction style to create a more immersive and realistic experience.
2. Target Audience:
Fluentia is designed for individuals who want to improve their speaking confidence and
communication skills through interactive AI conversations.
The primary target users include:
• Students who want to practice presentations, recitations, and academic discussions.
• Job seekers who need to prepare for job interviews and professional conversations.
• Professionals who want to improve workplace communication, meetings, and client
interactions.
• Individuals with speaking anxiety who want a safe environment to practice conversations
without fear of judgment.
• Language learners who want to practice speaking and improve their conversational
confidence.
By providing a safe and accessible environment for practicing conversations, Fluentia helps
users develop stronger communication skills that are useful in academic, professional, and
everyday situations.

3. Emerging Technologies:
Artificial Intelligence (AI) - Artificial Intelligence powers the conversational capabilities of the
system, enabling the AI agent to generate realistic responses during roleplay scenarios and
analyze user interactions.
Voice AI Agent Technology - The application utilizes voice AI agents (such as systems powered
by voice APIs like Vapi or Gemini) to enable real-time voice conversations between the user and
the AI communication coach.

4. Ethical Considerations:
User Privacy and Data Security
User voice data and conversation sessions will be handled securely. The system will implement
encryption and secure data storage to protect sensitive user information.
Transparency and User Control
Users will be informed about how their conversation data is used for analysis. The application
will provide options for users to manage or delete their stored data.
Responsible AI Development
The AI system will be designed to provide constructive and supportive feedback without
discrimination based on accent, language background, or communication style. Feedback
generated by the system will focus on communication improvement rather than judging the user's
personal speaking characteristics.
Prevention of Misuse
Fluentia will be positioned strictly as a communication practice tool and will not replace
professional training, coaching, or psychological support.

5. Project Deliverables:
• A fully functional AI communication coaching web application
• Scenario-based AI conversation system
• AI-powered feedback and communication analysis module
• Communication scoring and progress tracking dashboard
• Conversation history and review feature
• AI persona-based interaction system

6. Potential Impact:
Fluentia can help individuals build confidence in speaking and improve their communication
skills through accessible AI-based practice.
The application can:

• Help students practice conversations and presentations
• Assist job seekers in preparing for interviews
• Support professionals in improving workplace communication
• Provide a safe and judgment-free environment for practicing speaking skills
By offering AI-powered conversation practice and feedback, Fluentia can make communication
training more accessible and scalable.
